
# CyberOrganism

CyberOrganism is a tool for real-time simulation and monitoring of neural network agents in a reinforcement learning environment. These agents, called 'organisms', learn behaviors to help them survive, like finding food and avoiding predators. The ever-changing environment provides a complex set of challenges, encouraging robust, generalizable, and adaptive behavior. Learning happens in real-time, allowing users to observe the training process in a way that is not possible with 'offline learning'. 

Just watching the organisms interact with their environment is fascinating in its own right, but CyberOrganism also comes with a suite of tools for profiling neural networks, monitoring training statistics, and adjusting simulation parameters. Users may choose to design novel neural network architectures for their organisms, or opt to let evolution guide the design process.

CyberOrganism is a platform with as many use cases as there are users. From academic research in artificial intelligence and complex systems to educational demonstrations of evolutionary principles, and even artistic explorations of digital life, CyberOrganism provides a powerful and flexible toolkit for exploring the fascinating world of artificial life and machine learning.

## Key Features and Capabilities

### Real-Time Simulation
- Watch organisms learn and adapt in a dynamic, 2D environment
- Observe the immediate effects of environmental changes on organism behavior
- Pause, speed up, or slow down the simulation to focus on specific learning moments

### Organism Interaction
- See organisms navigate their environment, searching for food and avoiding dangers
- Witness emergent behaviors as organisms learn to compete or cooperate
- Track individual organism stats like energy levels, nutrition, and lifespan

### Learning Visualization
- View real-time graphs of key learning metrics such as loss, reward, and Q-values
- See the attention mechanism of organisms in action as they focus on different parts of their environment
- Observe how exploration vs. exploitation strategies change over time

### Customization Options
- Adjust environmental parameters like food scarcity, predator presence, or obstacle density
- Modify organism attributes such as movement speed, sensory range, or metabolism rate
- Fine-tune learning hyperparameters including learning rate, discount factor, or network architecture

### Evolution and Natural Selection
- Enable evolutionary mechanisms to see how organism populations change over time
- Observe the effects of different selection pressures on organism traits and behaviors
- Experiment with mutation rates and inheritance mechanisms

### Analysis Tools
- Use built-in profiling tools to analyze neural network performance
- Export detailed logs and statistics for further analysis
- Compare different organism designs or learning strategies side-by-side

### Extensibility
- Design and implement your own neural network architectures
- Create custom environmental challenges or organism types
- Develop and test new reinforcement learning algorithms in a visual, intuitive setting

### Educational Value
- Ideal for students and educators in fields like AI, biology, or complex systems
- Provides an intuitive, visual introduction to concepts in reinforcement learning and evolutionary algorithms
- Encourages experimentation and hypothesis testing in a controlled environment

CyberOrganism offers a unique blend of visual appeal, scientific rigor, and user customization. Whether you're a researcher looking to test new AI algorithms, a student learning about reinforcement learning, or simply someone fascinated by artificial life, CyberOrganism provides a rich, interactive platform for exploration and discovery.
